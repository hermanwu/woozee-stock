About
Mission
Invesdea is founded in 2022 by engineers passionate about stock investing and software building.
Investing is a life-long journey, and it could be bumpy. We believe our tools and services can help anyone achieve their financial freedom more quickly and smoothly.
Milestones and Goals
1. Provide latest and most important financial news.
2. Crowd-source expert opinions and market sentiment.
3. Create a community of investors to share ideas and learn from each.
4. Build customizable tools tailored to every individual investor.
