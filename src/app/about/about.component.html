Mission
Woozee Investment is founded in 2022 by engineers passionate about stock investing and software building.
Investing is a life-long journey, and it could be bumpy. We believe our tools and services can help anyone achieve their financial freedom more quickly and smoothly.
Milestones and Goals
1. Provide the latest news and critical financial data.
2. Generate advanced insights using technology such as big data analytics, infographic visualization, and machine learning.
3. Build customizable tools tailored to every individual investor.
4. Create a platform for sharing and crowdsourcing ideas.
